Admissions

At H. M. Education Centre, we strive to provide quality education to nurture students into responsible and capable individuals. Admission is open to all children irrespective of caste, creed, or religion, subject to availability of seats.

Admission Process

1

Obtain the admission form from the school office.

2

Fill out the form carefully with all required details.

3

Submit the completed form along with the required documents.

4

Attend the interaction/entrance test.

5

Selected candidates will be notified and required to complete the fee payment.

Eligibility & Documents Required

  • Birth Certificate (original + photocopy).
  • Previous school report card (for applicable classes).
  • Transfer Certificate (for students seeking admission in higher classes).
  • Two passport size photographs of the student.
  • Photographs of parents/guardians.

Important Dates

Forms Available: March 1st onwards

Last Date of Submission: March 15th

Entrance Test / Interaction: March 20th

Publication of Results: March 25th
